Breakfast Margarita

Description

This breakfast margarita is the freshest, most delicious margarita you can imagine, even better than a mimosa for brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces tequila
  • 1/2 fluid ounce orange liqueur, such as Cointreau
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 2 teaspoons orange marmalade
  • orange slice, for garnish

Steps

  1. Fill a shaker halfway with ice; pour in tequila, orange liqueur, orange juice, and lime juice. Shake until the outside of the shaker is frosted, 15 to 30 seconds. Pour into a glass; garnish with an orange slice.
